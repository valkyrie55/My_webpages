    Are momos healthy? [Calorie calculation while eating momos]

    Let us count the calories we consume in veg/non veg momo -- 6 pieces veg steamed momos (90 gm): 270 Cal, 6 pieces non-veg steamed momos (150 gm): 390 cal and 2 tbsp (30 gm) Chilli sauce 30 cal. The overall calorie count is not too bad (45 kcal per piece) as long as you don't go wild with your quantities Here are tips to cut down calories while relishing momos at roadside and make it healthier. Try to cultivate a taste for veg momos, instead of the chicken ones. If you prefer chicken, mix them up. Have three each of veg and chicken momos. You will save 20 cal. per piece.
